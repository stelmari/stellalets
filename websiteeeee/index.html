Welcome to Cebu City Government

ONLINE SERVICES

Cebu City online services is a digital platform that provides convenient and efficient access to various services in Cebu City. This user-friendly platform aims to streamline administrative processes, save time, and enhance the overall convenience of accessing essential services for the people of Cebu City.

Department of Manpower Development & Placement

Efficient manpower services that cater to job placements and vocational training for the citizens of Cebu City, empowering individuals with the skills and opportunities necessary to thrive in today’s competitive job market. Our services connect job seekers with reliable employers while providing training programs tailored to meet the needs of various industries. By fostering a skilled workforce, we aim to contribute to the economic development of Cebu City and improve the quality of life for its residents through accessible employment opportunities and skill enhancement.
